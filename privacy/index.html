Privacy Policy

We collect the minimum data necessary to operate Praeon and deliver compliance pilots.

What we collect

  • Contact form submissions (name, email, org, use case, message).
  • Operational logs required to secure the Praeon Kernel.
  • Optional privacy-first analytics without cross-site tracking.

How we use data

Data powers compliance pilots, customer support, and platform security. We don’t sell personal data or run ads.

Retention

Operational traces stay encrypted at rest; audit traces are redacted for export. Contact submissions retain for 12 months unless requested sooner.

Your rights

Email privacy@praeon.ai to access, correct, or delete personal data. We honor GDPR/DPDP requirements.
