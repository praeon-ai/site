Privacy Policy

Last updated: 31 Oct 2025

Summary

We collect the minimum necessary information to operate this website and respond to inquiries. We don’t sell personal data. We don’t run third-party ads. We use privacy-respecting analytics.

What we collect

  • Browsing metadata (via our host/CDN): IP address, user-agent, pages visited, and timestamps for security and performance. Stored briefly.
  • Emails you send us (e.g., hello@praeon.ai, designpartners@praeon.ai): your email address and message content.
  • Optional web analytics (privacy-first; no cross-site tracking, no third-party ads).

How we use data

  • Operate, secure, and improve the site.
  • Respond to messages and support requests.
  • Detect abuse and prevent fraud.

Retention

  • Server/CDN logs: typically ≤ 90 days unless needed for security investigations.
  • Emails: kept as long as necessary to support our relationship or as required by law.

Your choices & rights

Depending on your jurisdiction (e.g., EU/EEA GDPR, India DPDP), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your data. To exercise these rights, contact privacy@praeon.ai.

Sharing

We share data with service providers who help us run this site (e.g., hosting/CDN, email). They act under contract and only as instructed by us. We may disclose information where required by law.

International transfers

Our infrastructure and providers may process data in multiple countries. We use reasonable safeguards for cross-border transfers.

Contact

Questions about privacy? Email privacy@praeon.ai.
