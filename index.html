Why now

Large enterprises and regulated startups need agents to close loops on refunds, case updates, and outreach. Security and compliance teams block write access when controls are unclear. Praeon supplies the governance substrate so you can turn writes on without months of review.

How it Works

Plan

Agents propose typed PlanLang action plans that list actors, systems, constraints, and spend.

Pre-Flight

The kernel evaluates policy, runs static checks, and performs a dry-run before any write.

Approve & Execute

Human or automated reviewers approve scopes, then adapters execute with least-privilege credentials.

Trace & Replay

Praeon captures replayable traces so you can investigate, red-team, or satisfy regulators on demand.

Compliance & Security

  • Secrets never land in traces—keys stay in HSM or KMS and are fetched only at execution.
  • Every adapter ships as a vendor-attested build with Sigstore/OCI digests and reproducible packaging.
  • Two-tier tracing keeps operational data encrypted while audit views stay redacted and shareable.
  • Built-in retention controls satisfy DPDP/GDPR and stream key events into your SIEM.
