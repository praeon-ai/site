What is MWAL-1?

  • Signed manifest (C2PA/CycloneDX/SPDX profile)
  • Verify before load with atomic FD semantics
  • Policy gates (who/what/where) at load-time
  • Quorum-signed manifests · rapid CRL
  • Audit receipts: JWS-signed, exportable, TTL retention

Targets: 1 GB decision P95 ≤ 200 ms; CRL propagation < 60 s.

Quick start

Sandbox only. For production, use signed packages/Helm in docs.

# verify & load with MWAL-1 (sandbox)
praeon gate verify \
  --manifest ./model.mwal.json \
  --policy ./policy.hcl \
  --runtime vllm \
  --load ./weights.bin
