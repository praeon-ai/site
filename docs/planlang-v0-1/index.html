Schema Highlights

  • Actors with typed identities (agent, human, service).
  • Capabilities mapped to adapters with least-privilege scopes.
  • Constraints for budget, risk, and temporal controls.
  • Steps enumerating preconditions and semantic effects.
  • Approvals referencing policy URIs and required roles.
