Compliance-Grade Action Audit Infrastructure

The substrate that lets agents act with confidence—typed plans, enforced policies, and audit-ready traces.

PlanLang (Target IR)

PlanLang is a human-auditable JSON schema covering capabilities, guardrails, approvals, and budgets so risk teams can greenlight work before agents touch production.

Praeon Kernel (Reference Runtime)

The Praeon Kernel performs pre-flight static checks, enforces policy, executes with least privilege, and powers semantic replay when you need to investigate.

Adapters

Email · Slack/Teams · Salesforce · Jira/ServiceNow · GitHub/GitLab · Payments (Stripe/Razorpay). Every adapter ships as a vendor-attested, sandboxed build.

Traces

Operational traces stay encrypted for engineering. Audit traces are redacted, canonicalized, replayable, and ready for regulators or the Big Four.

Deployments

Run Praeon as managed SaaS, on-prem, or sovereign. Integrate with Okta or Entra for identity, Splunk or Datadog for monitoring, and your preferred KMS or HSM for keys.
