Security

Last updated: 31 Oct 2025 · /.well-known/security.txt

Principles

  • Attestation first: nothing runs without proof.
  • Evidence by default: every admitted load emits a signed receipt.
  • Revocation that matters: CRL + transparency log with fast propagation.

Report a vulnerability

Email security@praeon.ai. Include impact, steps to reproduce, affected URLs/versions, and PoC if available. For sensitive reports, use our PGP key.

Acknowledgement within 24h. Remediation plan within 3 business days for critical issues. Safe Harbor: Good-faith research in scope is welcome.

Scope

  • In scope: praeon.ai & subdomains; Praeon Gate/Ledger/Suite pre-release builds shared with partners.
  • Out of scope: third-party providers, physical/social engineering, DoS, spam, rate-limit tests.

Transport & headers (proof)

  • HTTPS only; TLS 1.2+ (1.3 preferred)
  • DNSSEC enabled
  • HSTS: preload planned post-stability
  • Baseline headers: Strict-Transport-Security, Content-Security-Policy, X-Content-Type-Options: nosniff, Referrer-Policy
$ curl -s -D- https://praeon.ai -o /dev/null strict-transport-security: max-age=31536000; includeSubDomains content-security-policy: default-src 'self' x-content-type-options: nosniff referrer-policy: no-referrer-when-downgrade
