Security

Last updated: 31 Oct 2025

Principles

  • Attestation first: nothing runs without proof.
  • Evidence by default: every admitted load emits an audit receipt.
  • Revocation that matters: CRL + transparency log with fast propagation.

Contact

Report security issues to security@praeon.ai. For sensitive reports, use our PGP key (coming soon) and include steps to reproduce, impact, and any logs.

We acknowledge within 24 hours and provide a remediation plan within 3 business days for critical issues.

/.well-known/security.txt Contact: mailto:security@praeon.ai Policy: https://praeon.ai/security Preferred-Languages: en

Vulnerability Disclosure Policy (VDP)

We welcome good-faith research. Please:

  • Avoid privacy violations and data destruction.
  • Don’t disrupt service (no DoS, no spam, no automated volumetric tests).
  • Give us reasonable time to remediate before public disclosure.

Safe Harbor. We won’t pursue legal action for research carried out in good faith and within this policy’s scope.

Scope

  • In scope: praeon.ai and subdomains we operate; Praeon Gate, Ledger, Suite pre-release builds provided to partners.
  • Out of scope: third-party services (cloud providers, registrars), social engineering, physical attacks, denial-of-service.

Transport & Headers

  • HTTPS only; TLS 1.2+ (1.3 preferred)
  • HSTS (preload planned post-stability)
  • DNSSEC enabled
  • Security headers baseline: Strict-Transport-Security, Content-Security-Policy (tightened over time), X-Content-Type-Options: nosniff, Referrer-Policy: no-referrer-when-downgrade
