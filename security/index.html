Security & Compliance

Praeon delivers audit-grade controls across typed plans, least-privilege execution, and replayable traces.

Token Handling

Secrets never appear in traces. Adapters fetch credentials from customer HSM/KMS at execution time and discard immediately after use.

Vendor-Attested Adapters

Adapters ship as reproducible builds signed with Sigstore/OCI digests. Customers can verify provenance before enabling capabilities.

Sandboxing & Egress

Praeon Kernel enforces deterministic sandboxing, outbound allowlists, and network policy per adapter. Semantic replay executes in isolated environments.

Retention Controls

DPDP/GDPR retention policies govern operational and audit traces. Automatic deletion policies are enforced via customer-managed KMS expiration.

SIEM & Observability

Plan validation, approval events, executions, and replay runs emit structured events for Splunk, Datadog, and other SIEM platforms.

Contact Security

Email security@praeon.ai for coordinated disclosure. Response targets: acknowledge in 24h, remediation plan in 3 business days for critical issues.
